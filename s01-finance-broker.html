Frequently Asked Questions

How is your service free? ‚ñº

When we place you with a lender, the lender pays us a commission. It's standard in the industry. Your rate isn't inflated because of this‚Äîthe lender pays us from their profit margin. You pay nothing extra. In fact, because we compare 60+ lenders, we often find you a better rate than you'd get by approaching the bank directly.

Will checking my mortgage with you affect my credit score? ‚ñº

No. Our initial consultation and rate comparison are done without a credit check. We only run your credit with your explicit permission when you've decided to move forward with a specific lender. A single credit inquiry has minimal impact and disappears in 12 months. Multiple applications within 14 days count as one inquiry, so we time them strategically.

How long does the whole process take? ‚ñº

Our strategy call is 30 minutes. We then spend 1-2 weeks comparing options and gathering your documentation. Once you've chosen a lender and approved our application, approval typically takes 3-5 business days. Settlement happens 4-6 weeks after approval. The entire process from call to money in your account is usually 6-8 weeks. Refinancing can be faster‚Äîsometimes 3-4 weeks total.

What if I'm self-employed or have irregular income? ‚ñº

Self-employed borrowers are our speciality. Many mainstream banks reject self-employed applicants outright. We work with specialist lenders who understand business income, tax strategies, and investment structures. We'll help you present your financials in the best light. Most self-employed clients are approved within the same timeline as traditional borrowers.

Are you able to help with first-home buyer grants and schemes? ‚ñº

Yes. We specialise in first-home buyer strategy. We know which lenders offer the best terms for first-time buyers, how to navigate stamp duty exemptions (Australia), and how to maximise shared ownership schemes (UK). We also know which grants you might be eligible for and how to structure your purchase to access them.

Can you help with investment property loans? ‚ñº

Absolutely. Investment loans are more complex than home loans, and many mainstream banks don't specialise in them. We work with lenders who understand property portfolios, negative gearing, depreciation scheduling, and multi-property strategies. We'll help you structure your loans to maximise tax efficiency and build wealth faster.
