## The Cornerstone Conversion Method™

Every landing page follows this proven psychological sequence:

1. **Pattern Break** — Bold headline that stops the scroll
2. **Ideal Client Call-Out** — "This is for YOU" identification
3. **Problem Agitation** — Twist the knife on pain points
4. **Solution Reveal** — Present the product/service as the answer
5. **Authority & Social Proof** — Testimonials, press, results
6. **The Irresistible Stack™** — An offer so compelling they can't say no
7. **Risk Reversal** — Guarantees that remove all friction
8. **Urgency & Scarcity** — Time or quantity limited
9. **FAQ / Objection Handling** — Eliminate final doubts
10. **Final CTA** — Clear, compelling call to action
