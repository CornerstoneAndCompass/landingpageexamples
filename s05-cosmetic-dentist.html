We offer flexible finance options with 0% interest available. Treatments can be spread over months or years, making your dream smile genuinely affordable. Our team will discuss all your options during your free consultation and find a plan that works for your budget.

This depends on your treatment. Some treatments like teeth whitening take a single appointment. Veneers typically require two visits. Invisalign takes 12-18 months with regular appointments. During your consultation, we'll give you a detailed timeline for your specific treatment plan.
