Questions About Your Custom Piece

How long does the custom process take? +

From consultation to delivery, the timeline typically spans 8-12 weeks. This includes the initial consultation (1 week), design iterations (2-3 weeks), your final approval (1 week), and handcrafting (4-6 weeks). Rush options are available for additional investment.

What metals do you work with? +

We exclusively use 18K gold (white, yellow, and rose), platinum, and palladium. All gold is either ethically mined or recycled. We do not work with lower karat golds or imitation metals. Your piece is an investment meant to last generations.

How do you source gemstones? +

Every diamond and precious gemstone is sourced through verified suppliers who maintain full chain-of-custody documentation. All diamonds meet Kimberley Process standards, and we work exclusively with conflict-free stones. Certification from GIA is standard for all diamonds over 0.5 carats.

What's the pricing structure? +

Pricing varies based on materials, complexity, and stone selection. Custom engagement rings range $3,500-$15,000+. Statement necklaces start at $2,000. We provide detailed quotes after the design phase is complete. A 50% deposit secures your spot; the balance is due upon completion.

Can you resize existing jewelry? +

Absolutely. Whether your piece is from Maison Aurelie or elsewhere, we offer professional resizing at competitive rates. For Maison Aurelie pieces, resizing is complimentary as part of our Forever Promise. Bring your piece in during business hours or ship it to us securely.

Do you offer insurance recommendations? +

We absolutely recommend insuring your custom piece. We'll provide a detailed appraisal document for insurance purposes at the time of completion. We partner with several reputable jewelry insurers and can provide referrals. Appraisal insurance typically costs 1-3% of the piece's value annually.
