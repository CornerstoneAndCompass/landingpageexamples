Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know.

Do I need planning permission?

+

It depends on what you're building and where. Most extensions, loft conversions, and renovations fall under "Permitted Development" if they meet certain criteria, meaning you won't need formal permission. However, some projects do require it. During your free design session, we'll assess your specific situation and advise you exactly what's required. We handle all planning applications and approvals‚Äîit's included in our service.

How long will my project take?

+

Timelines vary based on scope and complexity. A kitchen renovation might take 8‚Äì12 weeks. An extension could be 14‚Äì20 weeks. A full house renovation might be 20‚Äì30 weeks. We give you a fixed timeline during the quote phase‚Äîand we hit it. Our 98% on-time delivery record speaks for itself. We build in contingency where it matters and communicate any changes immediately.

Can I stay in my home during the work?

+

Yes. Most of our clients stay home during renovations. We manage dust, noise, and access carefully. For large-scale work, we'll recommend temporary protection measures (like dust barriers) to minimise disruption. For full house renovations or periods of significant structural work, temporary relocation might make sense‚Äîwe'll advise you on what works best for your situation and can help coordinate arrangements if needed.

What warranty do you provide?

+

Every project comes with a 12-month defect warranty. Any issues with materials, workmanship, or installation that appear within 12 months of handover are our responsibility‚Äîwe fix them at no cost. For structural work, we provide an NHBC warranty which extends protection for up to 10 years on certain elements. You'll receive full documentation covering all warranties when we complete your project.

What's the payment schedule?

+

We use a staged payment approach: 25% deposit to secure your start date and materials, 50% at key milestones throughout the project, and the final 25% upon satisfactory handover. This protects both of us and ensures we're incentivised to deliver on schedule. For larger projects (¬£100k+), we can discuss flexible payment structures that work for your cash flow. All details are clearly outlined in your fixed-price contract.

Do you offer eco-friendly & sustainable options?

+

Absolutely. We specialise in sustainable renovations including: high-performance insulation, renewable energy systems (solar PV, heat pumps), sustainable materials, water-efficient fixtures, and energy-efficient heating. Building regulations now require better energy performance, and we exceed these standards. We'll discuss eco options during your design consultation and include them in your quote. Investing in sustainability can reduce your energy bills significantly‚Äîwe'll model the financial return for you.
